Purpose

Stock Trainer is paper trading of share market with real-time data. You can simulate buy & sell share of more than 8000+ stocks listed in National Stock Exchange and Bombay Stock Exchange. We use real data of markets which help beginners to do actual practice and gain real-life experience without investing any real money.

This app is for perfect for everyone who wants to do paper trading no matter if you are beginners or professional.

For beginners, you can learn and gain experience here with nothing to lose in reality.

For professionals, you can keep track of portfolio which you might have invested in different stock broker's platform or check if your guess at any stock is right or wrong.
